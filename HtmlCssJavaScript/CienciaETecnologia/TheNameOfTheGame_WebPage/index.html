Mario Kart

Mario Kart is Going to go Down as One of the Most Legendary Games in History!

When you play the game mario kart you remember what is like to be a kid again. Inviting your friends to your house and bonding through a incredible game.

But really Mario Kart is much more than just a nostalgic game that generates incredible memories.

What Makes Mario Kart so Great?

A recurrent theme in this webpage will be the repetitive mentions of a game doing it's best to achieve perfection.

Mario Kart is simply a game that was refined to perfection have at the very list a dozen of different versions and a collection of games bigger then most franchises.

The gameplay is simply superb! You will never feel like playing another race game after you play Mario Kart! The movement is wonky at first, but as you get good at the game it becomes easier and easier to master. After the results are pretty satisfying! The other mechanics like bumping on other players, falling of the arena and throwing randomized shit at your oponents just makes the game that more fun!

So... Should You Play Mario Kart?

Yes, yes, yes! Mario Kart is a very friendly video game that warms up the cold hearts of the most avid non gamers!

Just dedicated your self to getting used to the game''s movements and a garantuee the amount of fun will be unlimited!

Mortal Kombat

Mortal of Kombat is Just the Meaning of Perfection When it Comes to Fighting Games!

Sometimes you just want to fucking go at it with your friends! After we have to know who is a fucking a fucking LOSER and who is not.

Since the beggining of the franchise the game was always renowed and loved by it's community of players.

Since then things have just gotten better and better!

The Best Fighting Game Mechanics

Something that is absolutely striking in Mortal Kombat is how balance the game is. You are talking about a game where a D tier character has real chances of winning against a S tier character!

Once again the quality of the game came from years of existance and multiple game's in the franchise that have the same exact objective. Allowing the game to objectively improve as the years go by.

Should You Play Mortal Kombat?

Mortal Kombat is a very niche games. Most people are not big fans of the gore and fighting games are already niche as it is.

The whole point of the game is to reward players that are gifted mechanically. Therefore the mechanics will definitely feel to complex and frustrating for most people.

Conclusion: if you feel interested in fighting games, then yes. Otherwise not really.

Zelda

Zelda is the Biggest Mario Classic

I Love Zelda! What a unbelivable classic!

The whole point of the game is to reward players that are gifted mechanically. Therefore the mechanics will definitely feel to complex and frustrating for most people.

But Makes Zelda so Great?

Zelda is a game that will enwrap you in a beautiful world of fantasy. There really is no other way of saying it, the story is just THAT captivating!

If I could go back to my childhood Zelda would be the first game that I would play.

It is simply one of the best stories in gaming history.

Should You Play Zelda?

Most people will love it! But there will be a minority of people that will dislike it.

The style of the game is very childish and the strong suits of the game are just not really pleasant for everybody. I would rather you try it out and see it for yourself.
